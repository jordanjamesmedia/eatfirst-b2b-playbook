Full-Funnel Campaign Strategy

Structured approach from awareness to conversion

Awareness (TOFU)

Video Ads Thought Leadership 8-10% Lookalikes 20-30% Budget

Consideration (MOFU)

Showcase menu options, pricing transparency, and ease of ordering. Target engaged users from awareness campaigns.

Carousel Ads Menu Showcases 2-5% Lookalikes 30-40% Budget

Drive quote requests with testimonials, limited-time offers, and frictionless lead forms.

"Get Your Free Quote in 24 Hours"

  • Warm retargeting: Quote page visitors, menu viewers

Lookalike Strategy

  • 1% lookalike of best customers (highest LTV)
  • 1% lookalike of lead form completers
  • 2-5% lookalikes for scale testing
  • Value-based lookalikes using order value

Geographic Targeting Recommendation

Focus on downtown business districts, commercial office parks, and corporate centers within your delivery radius. Target specific zip codes with high commercial density for maximum relevance and efficient ad spend.

Video Best Practices

  • Keep under 15 seconds
  • Show food prep & packaging
  • Add captions (85% watch muted)
  • 5 short videos > 1 long video

Carousel Best Practices

  • 30-50% lower cost per conversion
  • Tell a story across cards
  • Showcase different cuisines
  • Consistent aspect ratios

Copy Guidelines

  • Lead with benefits, not features
  • Include social proof
  • Clear CTAs: "Get Quote"
  • Address pain points directly

Implementation Roadmap

12-week launch and optimization plan

Weeks 1-2 | Foundation
Setup & Configuration
  • Install Meta Pixel on all website pages
  • Configure Conversion API (CAPI) with CRM
  • Build custom audiences from existing customer data
  • Create 1% lookalikes from best customers
  • Develop 3-5 creative variations per funnel stage
Weeks 3-6 | Testing
Campaign Launch & Testing
  • Launch Awareness campaign (video content) - $20-30/day
  • Launch Consideration campaign (carousels, lead magnets) - $30-40/day
  • Launch Conversion campaign (lead forms, quotes) - $40-50/day
  • Use ABO structure for audience/creative testing
  • Monitor and adjust weekly based on data
Weeks 7-10 | Optimization
Scale Winners, Cut Losers
  • Identify winning audiences and creatives
  • Pause underperforming ad sets
  • Increase budget on winners by 20-30%
  • Transition to CBO structure for scaling
  • Implement advanced retargeting campaigns
Weeks 11+ | Scaling
Growth & Expansion
  • Scale budgets 20-30% weekly on successful campaigns
  • Expand lookalike audiences (2-5%)
  • Test new creative angles and messaging
  • Add seasonal campaigns (holidays, events)
  • Continuous A/B testing for optimization
